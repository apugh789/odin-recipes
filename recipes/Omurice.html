Omurice

Ingredients

  • Chicken rice ... about 400g
  • Eggs ... 4 whole
  • Your preferred oil ... 2 teaspoons
  • A Ketchup ... 4 Tablespoons
  • A Water ... 1 1/2 Tablespoons
  • A Butter ... 10g

作り方

  1. Scramble two eggs and add about 2 pinches of salt.
  2. Heat the skillet to medium heat and add the oil.
  3. Pour the eggs in.
  4. Once the eggs are half cooked, turn off the heat.
  5. Place about half of the rice (200g) on the eggs. (Place the rice slightly to the further side from the handle.)
  6. Fold the egg mixture over the rice. (Fold from the side closest to the handle)
  7. In the shape of a taco, move the ommlette to a plate.
  8. With a papertowel, fold the ommeltte to where the opening crease is on the bottom.

Omurice ketchup sauce

  1. Add the A ingredients to a sauce pan.
  2. Cook over low heat, and once the butter melts you are finished.
