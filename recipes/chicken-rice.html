Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • Warm rice ... 400g
  • Chicken thighs ... 75g
  • Onion ... 100g (If it's a small onion use 1/2)
  • Carrot ... 50g (About 1/3 of a large carrot)
  • Olive oil ... 1/2 Tablespoons
  • A Ketchup ... 3 Tablespoons
  • A (If you have it) Tomato paste ... 1 Tablespoon
  • A Butter ... 10g
  • B Salt ... 1/4 teaspoon
  • B Pepper ... Just a pinch

作り方

  1. Dice everything.
  2. Put the veggies and meat in the skillet and cook.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Mix in the A ingredients.
  5. Add the rice in.
  6. Lastly, add in the B ingredients.
