Dry Curry

Ingredients

  • Hot Rice
  • Preferred Ground Meat (Chicken, Pork, Beef, etc)
  • Onion
  • Green Peppers
  • Egg Plant
  • Corrot
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • A Salt
  • A Curry Powder
  • A Pepper
  • B Ketchup
  • B Soy Sauce
  • B Mirin
  • B Sugar (optional)

作り方

  1. Cut Everything up.
  2. Sautee the garlic and ginger.
  3. Add the eggplant, carrot, and onion.
  4. Add the green peppers.
  5. Add the meat.
  6. Once the meat is cooked, add the A ingredients.
  7. After that add the B ingredients.
